WELCOME

COMPLECCITY is a research lab, based in department of Building, Civil and Environmental Engineering at Concordia University - Montreal, focusing on optimized solutions for Construction, Operation and Management of the built environment (city infrastructure and building systems).

COMPLECCITY

Applications of Artificial Intelligence (AI), big data analytics, Building Information Modeling (BIM) and Urban Information Modeling for digital twinning of the built environment are at the core of our research mission. The diverse academic background of members and researchers in the team allows for a wide range of strategies to accomplish these resolutions.

COMPLECCITY

The COMPLECCITY team is comprised of highly motivated, passionate and ambitious individuals who are enthusiastic about advancement of knowledge, high impact research, and development of state-of-the-art cross-disciplinary solutions for existing problems of the built environment. We embrace diversity and inclusion and are welcoming of like-minded new members.,
